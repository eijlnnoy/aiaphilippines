Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is AIA Philippines and why choose it?+
AIA Philippines is one of the country's leading life insurers with decades of financial strength, a wide product range, and innovative wellness programs like AIA Vitality. It holds one of the highest financial strength ratings in the industry.
How do I get a personalized quotation?+
Tap any "Inquire +" button or email quotation@insurancepilipinas.com. We'll respond within 24 hours with a personalized proposal. Consultations are 100% free.
What is AIA Med-Assist powered with AIA Vitality?+
AIA Med-Assist covers in-patient and out-patient health benefits. Powered by AIA Vitality, it rewards healthy living with discounts, cashback, and perks — the healthier you live, the more you save.
What does AIA Critical Protect 100 cover?+
It covers 100+ critical illnesses across multiple stages (early, intermediate, major). If you complete the policy without any claim, 100% of premiums are returned. Life coverage is also included.
What is the Future Scholar education plan?+
Future Scholar guarantees your child's education fund even if you pass away before finishing premium payments. Payouts happen at key school milestones so your child's education is never compromised.
Where can I watch free educational videos?+
Subscribe to @infinitewealthfinancials on YouTube for free breakdowns of all AIA products. We also share tips on Instagram (@insurancepilipinas), TikTok (@insurepilipinas), and LinkedIn.
