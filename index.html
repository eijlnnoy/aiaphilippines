FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about our products and services.

What is AIA Philippines and why should I choose it?+
AIA Philippines is one of the country's leading and most trusted life insurance companies, with decades of financial strength, a wide product range, and innovative wellness programs like AIA Vitality. It holds one of the highest financial strength ratings in the industry.
How do I get a personalized quotation?+
Simply click any "Add to Inquiry" button or email us at quotation@insurancepilipinas.com. We'll respond within 24 hours with a personalized proposal based on your needs, age, and budget. Consultations are completely free.
What is AIA Med-Assist powered with AIA Vitality?+
AIA Med-Assist is a comprehensive health insurance plan covering both in-patient and out-patient benefits. Powered by AIA Vitality, it rewards your healthy lifestyle with discounts, cashback, and partner perks — the healthier you live, the more you save on premiums.
What does AIA Critical Protect 100 cover?+
AIA Critical Protect 100 covers over 100 critical illnesses including cancer, heart attack, and stroke across multiple stages (early, intermediate, and major). If you complete the policy without any claim, 100% of your premiums are returned. It also includes life coverage.
What is the Future Scholar education plan?+
Future Scholar is an AIA education savings plan that guarantees your child's education fund even if you pass away before completing premium payments. The plan continues paying out at key educational milestones, so your child's schooling is never compromised.
Where can I watch free educational videos about these plans?+
Subscribe to our YouTube channel @infinitewealthfinancials where we publish free, simplified breakdowns of all AIA products. We also share tips and insights on Instagram (@insurancepilipinas), TikTok (@insurepilipinas), and LinkedIn.
